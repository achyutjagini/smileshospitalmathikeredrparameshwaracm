Real Review of Smiles Hospital Mathikere

A detailed account of medical negligence and patient safety concerns

Hospital & Doctor Information

  • Hospital: Smiles Hospital, Mathikere, Bengaluru
  • Doctor: Dr. Parameshwara C M
  • Treatment: Anal Fistula, Piles, Anal Fissure Surgery

Summary of Experience

What started as treatment for an anal fistula resulted in severe, life-altering fecal incontinence due to an unauthorized surgical procedure performed without consent.

Initial Consultation

I first visited Smiles Hospital for treatment of an anal fistula. Dr. Parameshwara also diagnosed piles and fissure requiring surgery. Though I had no complaints beyond the fistula, I was told surgery was necessary for all three conditions.

Pre-Surgery Discussion About Incontinence Risk

We specifically asked about fecal incontinence. The doctor and staff assured us:

  • No fecal incontinence risk
  • A sphincter-saving procedure would be used
  • Invasive fistulotomy would be avoided
  • They understood avoiding incontinence was our top priority

First Surgery - As Promised

The first surgery was minimally invasive and I recovered well with no incontinence issues. Everything seemed under control.

The Unauthorized Surgery

Deceptive EUA Recommendation: I was told it would only involve probe insertion with no surgery.

What Actually Happened: A full fistulotomy was performed without my knowledge or consent, leaving a large wound.

Life After Unauthorized Surgery

The fistulotomy caused severe fecal incontinence:

  • Loss of control over passing stools
  • Stools leaking without warning
  • Damaged anal sphincter muscle
  • No improvement since surgery

I now cannot go outside without diapers and cleaning supplies. It has devastated my daily life, mental health, and dignity.

Discovered Pattern of Concerning Practices

Research revealed other patients reported complications, misleading communication, unnecessary surgeries, and poor outcomes.

Additional Concerns

Deccan Herald articles about Dr. Parameshwara raise serious concerns:

  • 2019: Forgery charges
  • 2020: Molestation allegations by OT technician
